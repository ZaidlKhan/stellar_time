The Solar System
The solar system is a collection of celestial bodies, including the Sun, eight planets, their moons, and various comets, asteroids, and other space rocks.
The Milky Way, our home galaxy, spans over 100,000 light-years in diameter. A vast collection of stars, planets, gas, and dust, it's a spiral galaxy that contains our Solar System.

Fun Facts

  • The Solar System is around 4.6 billion years old.
  • There are 8 recognized planets in our Solar System.
  • The Kuiper Belt, beyond Neptune, is home to many icy bodies and dwarf planets, including Pluto.
